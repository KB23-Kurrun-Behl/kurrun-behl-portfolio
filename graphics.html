PRIME

The purpose of this design collection was to promote the five fantastic flavours
of the newly-released hydration drink by KSI & Logan Paul.

PRIME Hydration is a better-for-you option where function and flavor come first.
Each bottle contains 10% coconut water and zero sugar.

The flavors include Tropical Punch, Blue Raspberry and Grape.
